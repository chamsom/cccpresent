Hello and welcome to my presentation! My name is Justin, but you can also call me Jae! (Based on my given Korean name: 김재훈)

So who am I?

I am a graduate of Loyola University Chicago that majored in Computer Science and minored in Music with a piano performance concentration.

Upon graduating, I started to work for JPMorgan Chase & Co. as a full stack engineer for their corporate and investment banking team. I assisted in the development of several functions that automated the enrollment process for corporate credit cards. This is also where I learned a variety of financial instruments and products utilized by investors and traders!

After JPMorgan Chase & Co., I was hired on as a remote Solutions Engineer for a tech startup based in San Francisco known as InfluxDB. My previous responsibilities as a Solutions Engineer involved partnering with the Enterprise and Territory sales teams to maintain technical relationships with several Fortune 100 and Fortune 500 clients. I not only designed software solutions but also catered products specific to each customer use case. This experience allowed me to work closely and wear many hats with various interdisciplinary teams such as: engineering, marketing, product management, and sales.

What am I looking for in my next role?

I am looking to transition and make a lateral move into a product management role that will allow me to exercise my consulting background, provide data-driven approaches, and also work with industry leaders within cross-functional teams. A frustration that I had was not being able to to optimize or control the product I was working with. The lack of autonomy over the product roadmaps and designs often led to confusing intiatives and confused customers. I believe that the role will allow me exercise my curiosity in optimizing existing products for growth and present a wonderful opportunity to learn more about the P&C insurance economy.

With CCC Intelligent Solutions Inc. quite literally being the industry leader in this market, I am excited at the opportunity to manage the execution of internal operational and customer Go-to-Market readiness for product portfolios. The ability to present and communicate the vision, strategy, and roadmaps for the product management teams is something that I am very passionate about and hopefully looking forward to!
